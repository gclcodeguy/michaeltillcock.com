About Michael

TLDR: I worked as a Management Consultant in Financial Services for 8+ years, focussed on risk and technology change. In October 2022, I started building golfclublist.com, which aims to become a 'Compare the Market' like experience for all things golf.

1 - Management Consultant

I spent 8+ years working as a management consultant for KPMG, BCS Consulting and Accenture. I worked in Financial Services, solving change related problems for clients including HSBC, Barclays, Santander, RBS and First Abhu Dhabi Bank (FAB). I resigned as a Senior Manager at Accenture in May 2022, to pursue some other career ambitions and curiousities.

2- Aspiring Software Entrepreneur

Since October 2022, I have been following a long held ambition to learn to code, and build an internet business as an independent developer. My passion outside of work is golf, so I decided to make my first attempt in the business world related to golf, with golfclublist.com. My ambition for golfclublist.com is to build it into a revenue generating business, by providing useful, golf related insights for golfers and golf businesses.

3 - Content Creation

I co-host a golf podcast called Golf Social with my friend and fellow golf badger Toby Allodi. Since February 2022, I have gained over 5.5m views on my various social media channels, under the handle @MichaelJohnGolf, by creating golf related content and podcast clips. I started this in order to provide a low cost channel to market golfclublist.com and have subsequently found that I enjoy the creative process of making content, and learning how to gain engagement on various platforms.
